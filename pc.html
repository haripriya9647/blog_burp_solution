EITHER RUN THE DAY OR THE DAY RUNS YOU!




Author Priyanka Chakraborty

What is the toughest thing to control in life? The answers are many, but the most common one is ‘Time’. ‘Time and tide wait for none’. So, the secret to happiness is Effective Time Management. In other words, poor Time Management can make life equal to hell. So, how to control Time? Well, there are no set of procedures that can teach you the absolute meaning of Time Management. Yet, the following tips can provide a way to understand where to take a kick start.

1. LFirst of all, remember that Time Management is not a destination to be achieved; rather it is a journey to become better. So, taking Effective Time Management for granted, and settling down is not going to work. So, enjoy the journey of becoming better by managing your time now and then.

2. ‘Early to bed and early to rise’, a slogan learned from childhood days, is effective in practicing time management. When you wake up early, you can plan your ways and make schedules for your work. If you wake late, you will be entangled with what to do and what not. Therefore, leave your bed with sunrise.

3. ‘Jack of all trades and master of none’. It resembles involvement in several tasks, yet not able to complete a single one. So, don’t indulge yourself in various activities, and then struggle to reach the end of one even. Learn to say ‘NO’. Work according to your compatibility.

4. Stop procrastinating. Start doing. It’s hard to focus, if you are tired, relax; but don’t forget the invisible line between relaxation and Procrastination.

Time management may or may not be an innate skill, but not to worry, it can be learned. Don’t make unrealistic goals; try practicing some simple time management disciplines. Not only Time Management, but you can also learn any better habits by making some changes in your daily routines.
